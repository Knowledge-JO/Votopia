Votopia

Votopia is a decentralized voting application that provides a secure, tranparent and easy to use platform where members of the project can vote and ensure that budgets, fundings are allocated to available resources for the success of the project

Proposals

Research Projects

With Research Projects, we aim to foster innovation and explore new frontiers. Members can vote on proposals that focus on groundbreaking research initiatives, seeking to push the boundaries of knowledge in our industry. By supporting Research Projects, we invest in the future and nurture a culture of discovery within our organization.

Operational Expenses

Operational Expenses play a critical role in the day-to-day functioning of our organization. Voting on Operational Expenses proposals enables members to make decisions regarding the allocation of resources for essential activities such as infrastructure, staffing, and logistical needs. These decisions directly impact our operational efficiency and sustainability.

Capital Expenditure

Capital expenditures (CAPEX) involve significant investments in long-term assets that are essential for a company's operations. Voting on captital expenditure proposal enables members to make decisions regarding allocation of resources for essential activities such as purchase or upgrade Equipments, infrastructure, machinery which has a long term impact on the business.

Market Research

Market Research gives us a demographic information about the market of our product for gaining customers. Conducting market research helps us understand the demand, market size E.T.C of our product. Voting for Market Research proposal enables members to make decisions regarding the allocation of resources for conduction of market research which impacts our understanding of cusumer behaviour and improves our business idea.

Technological Advancments

Technological advancements allows us become cost efficient and effective, this will also provide a less seemless experience in handling day to day tasks allowing us save time and cost. Voting for technology Equipment proposal enables members to make decisions regarding the application of resources which will significantly impact our workflow and allow us better compete with established companies.

Human Resources

Human resources takes care of finding, recruiting, screening and training job applicants. voting for Human Resource proposal enables members to make decisions regarding the application of resources towards human resources to establish and improve the organization's workforce which will provide the company a long term benefit in terms of workforce in return allows us compete with already established companies.
